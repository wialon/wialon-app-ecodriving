Eco Driving module is intended to improve fleet safety, ensure cost-effective fleet maintenance, reduce fuel costs and enhance cargo safety. Flexible configuration of Eco Driving module allows to build a comprehensive and flexible driving quality assessment model and leverage data from hardware accelerometers.

The assessment model is based on six violation criteria, including abrupt acceleration, hard braking, sharp turns, speeding, reckless driving or custom violation with customizable coefficients set up individually.

Reduce fuel costs, ensure cargo safety, prolong vehicle life and rate up driving quality with Eco Driving module by Gurtam.
